Interactive Sketch Planner (Coming soon!)

Use this Interactive Sketch Planner to draw out each run. Be flexible. This plan might have to change during the season because once you start to build and program, you might find it easier to go a different route or combine missions in a different way. This tool will be available after the official challenge release.

EV3Lessons Trash Trek Scorer (Coming soon!)

Use our scorer to check your score throughout the season. This scorer will be available after the official challenge release.

Wheel Converter

You can use our wheel converter to convert centimeters/inches to degrees/rotations. Refer to the MyBlocks lessons in Intermediate.

Engineering Journal

Building and programming with robots is an iterative process. Document your trials using an Engineering Journal. Engineering Journals for First Lego League are not required.

Core Value Activities by FLL Starting Point

Consensus Loop, Have a Ball, Get in Line, Book Learning, Joined at the Hip, Losing Your Marbles, Tall Tower.

FLL Overview Presentation

This is a brief overview of FLL that might be useful to explain FLL to someone who is not familiar with it. It goes over all the three parts of the program. Feel free to use it at your outreach events. A variety of team photos are included, but feel free to change the images if you want to add pictures of your own team.
